F.A.Q

  • What is the eligibility criteria and cost for participation ?

    All team members should be currently enrolled students at any college, regardless of their branch or year of study. We encourage participants from various academic backgrounds, but we do expect all team members to demonstrate problem-solving abilities and the capability to craft innovative solutions.Cost of paticipation is Rs.399/- per team.

  • How many members can be in a team ?

    You must assemble a team comprising minimum 3 and maximum 5 members including the team leader. Minimum 1 girl is suggested but not compulsory. All team members should be currently enrolled students at any college, regardless of their branch or year of study.

  • What will be the location and timing for grand finale ?

    Our hackathon's grand finale will be held at UCER Campus, Prayagraj, on February 27th and 28th 2026. The event will run from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM on both days. Finalist will receive details and the event schedule closer to the date via emails, social media channels.

  • What should I bring to the event ?

    You need to carry your college ID Card, laptop, phone, chargers and a geeky t-shirt!
